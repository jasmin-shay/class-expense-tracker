About Class Pay

Class Pay is a class expense management system designed to reduce confusion between students and class leaders while collecting money for class activities.

The leader can create and assign expenses for the class, and students can easily view the amount they need to pay.

Students will be able to clearly see whether their payment is paid or unpaid, which helps maintain transparency in the class.

The system also keeps track of all small payments made by students and automatically calculates the total amount collected.

This makes it easier for the class leader to manage expenses and for students to know exactly how much has been contributed and how much is remaining.

This project was developed in 2026 as a mini project by students of the Computer Science Department, created by Jasmine and Group.
